===== Mozambique Information =====

Mozambique, officially the Republic of Mozambique, is a country located in southeastern Africa bordered by the Indian Ocean to the east, [[country:tanzania:home|Tanzania]] to the north, [[country:malawi:home|Malawi]] and [[country:zambia:home|Zambia]] to the northwest, [[country:zimbabwe:home|Zimbabwe]] to the west, and [[country:eswatini:home|Eswatini]] and [[country:south_africa:home|South Africa]] to the southwest. The sovereign state is separated from the [[country:comoros:home|Comoros]], [[country:mayotte:home|Mayotte]] and [[country:madagascar:home|Madagascar]] by the Mozambique Channel to the east. The capital and largest city is Maputo. 

For more information, please visit [[country:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozambique:home|Wikipedia]].
